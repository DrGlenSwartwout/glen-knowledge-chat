Harmonic Ladder
Overtone Series · Music of the Spheres
The fundamental's natural overtone series — partials at f₀, 2f₀, 3f₀ … 8f₀. Each rung's height is the FFT magnitude at that harmonic. A clean, well-supported voice shows a tall ladder with strong upper harmonics; a tight, breathy, or fatigued voice flattens above the 3rd or 4th rung.
The ladder is the Pythagorean architecture inside every sustained tone — octave (2:1), fifth (3:2), fourth (4:3), major third (5:4) unfold automatically from a single fundamental. Watching the upper rungs rise and fall is watching the coherence of your voice in real time. Spirit mineral: Gold (the noble center, shared with Fire and Metal — the integrating principle).
