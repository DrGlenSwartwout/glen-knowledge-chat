Part OneFor the Company

A health company is a coherence project before it is a commercial one. Every question below comes back to the same standard: are we restoring coherence in the people we serve, in the team that serves them, and in the community around us?

¶Mission statement — the largest picture, the highest aspiration

Mission
To restore coherence — in the body, in the practitioner, and in the community we serve — so that healing can outpace aging.

The model behind the words: health emerges from coherence across systems. When the biofield is coherent, the body reverses damage faster than it accumulates. Our work is to remove obstacles, supply what is missing, and let the body — which is 85% of the healing — do what it already knows how to do.

¶Purpose & goals — specifics in action

Equip every willing person — patient and practitioner alike — with the tools to reverse degenerative disease, recover lost vision, and rejoin the body’s own intelligence.

  • 12-month operating goals. Move from ~$300K/year to seven figures. Launch one new Functional Formulation™ per month from a 150-formulation R&D pipeline. Stand up a practitioner channel that multiplies one trained clinician into roughly fifty patients reached per month.
  • Outcome standard. Measured clinical breakthroughs, not symptom relief alone. If the numbers do not move, neither does the claim.
  • Reach standard. Whole-person care available at any tier — from a low-ticket AI coaching app at the front door, through Biofield Analysis™ consults and Remedy Match™ formulations, up to a high-ticket Health Business program for practitioners.

¶How the team learns the principles & gets on board

People are not hired into tasks here. They are hired into the worldview first, then trained on the tools. Onboarding always covers three anchors:

  • The foundational case. Glen’s own BEV reversal — biological age 68 to 33 in 36 months, eye pressure 28 down to 15, glaucoma reversed, 20/20 at 70 with no glasses. The proof case behind every claim we make.
  • The model. Coherence over suppression. Terrain over pathogen. The body heals itself; we remove obstacles and supply what is missing. The 5 Phases of Remedy Match™ — Energize, Rejuvenate, Regenerate, Cleanse, Balance — applied in individualized sequence.
  • The standard. If we would not put it into our own bodies, we do not put it into anyone else’s. If we cannot measure the change, we do not claim the change.

Weekly standups re-anchor on mission before metrics. The metrics are downstream of the alignment, not a substitute for it.

¶How we show the team they are key

  • Direct, named attribution. Wins are credited specifically — to clients, in copy, in payroll notes. No anonymous contributions.
  • Compensation ahead of demand. We pay people up before we ask them to scale up. Burnout costs more than wages.
  • Tools bought, not asked-for. If a person needs a piece of software, a license, a course, or an hour back on their calendar — we buy it without making them justify it twice.
  • Calendar protection. No one is asked to drop their own healing in order to serve someone else’s. Integrity of the principle starts inside the company.
  • Sovereignty. Each team member runs their own domain. Glen does not micromanage Rae’s books; Rae does not redesign Glen’s formulations. Trust is the operating system.

¶How we add to team & community — and how we apply our principles

  • Education before commerce. Every new product ships paired with a free education asset — a masterclass, an article, a voice-scan-based assessment — before it is sold. Customers can learn the model whether or not they ever buy a bottle.
  • The 4 C’s outreach. Caregivers, Coaches, Clinicians, Consultants — every audience gets the underlying clinical theory, not just the SKU list. One practitioner trained reaches the patients we will never personally meet.
  • The 5 C’s applied to ourselves first. Context, Container, Contents, Connections, Communication. Before asking coherence of clients we audit our own — finances, systems, team relationships, public communications — using the same model.
  • Free-tier access. A low-ticket AI coaching app and a public RAG chatbot let anyone meet the work at no cost. Ability to pay never gates the doorway.

¶What the community thinks of us — and how we know

Inbound signal    Forty-plus client testimonials across three confidence tiers; long-term-client word-of-mouth referrals now sit as our number two acquisition channel, behind speaking. We are featured in People, USA Today, The Science Times, and Dartmouth Alumni Magazine.

Outbound measurement    Post-consult outcome tracking, Biofield Analysis re-testing on long-term clients, and a dedicated public results capture at truly.vip/Results (written, audio, and video in one flow). A dashboard reads sentiment from inbound email, chatbot transcripts, and call notes daily.

The honest answer    Our community thinks of us as the people who did not give up on them after the conventional system did. That is not a tagline — it is what they say to us, unprompted, in their own words.

¶Short-term and long-term community impact

Short term (≤ 12 months)    Roughly ten-fold reach through the B2B practitioner channel — one optometrist or functional practitioner trained equals approximately fifty patients per month served. Vision loss reversed, not just slowed, in measurable cohorts. Hawai‘i serves as the living laboratory.

Long term (3–5 years)    Functional Formulations™ carried on Fullscript (≈125,000 practitioners). Clinical Theory™ of Everything taught as a full certification through Mentorship University. A documented, replicable pathway proving that biological age reversal is normal, not extraordinary — and that degenerative eye disease is not a life sentence.

Part TwoFor the Team Member

These are the questions every member of our team should be able to answer for themselves. Below are Glen’s own answers — written first-person — both as a model for the conversation and as an honest declaration to the people he works with.

?Is the universe (God) friendly?

Yes. Consciousness is primary, and the field responds to coherent intention. The evidence is in the body itself — the body cannot lie, and it heals when given the chance. A hostile universe does not reverse thirty-five years of biological age in thirty-six months. A friendly one does.

?Who are you, and what do you want out of life?

A naturopathic optometrist, a researcher, and — first — a steward of the people in front of me. What I want is that the work outlives me: that every practitioner who needs Clinical Theory has it, and that the next generation inherits a world where reversal is the default expectation, not the miracle.

?Why are you here?

To finish what started in 1982 — the unified clinical model that connects physics, biology, and consciousness — and to put it into the hands of the practitioners who will carry it past my lifetime. Every formulation, every protocol, every book is a delivery vehicle for that single transmission.

?What is your highest purpose to fulfill?

To be a transparent conduit. To let what wants to come through, come through — Functional Formulations™, Clinical Theory™, Wellness Whispering™ — and not get in its way with ego or attachment to outcome. The work is not mine. I am one of its current vehicles.

?What do you need to be fulfilled, and how do you give back?

Needs. Time to think. Time to write. Time to formulate. A team that runs the systems so attention stays on the science. A pace that protects sleep, sunlight, and the body’s own coherence.

Giving back. Every formulation released into the world. Every practitioner trained. Every patient who recovers vision they were told was permanently gone. The giving and the receiving are the same act — coherence flowing both directions.

?Is this a job — or a career and a way to happiness?

Neither. It is a vocation. The work and the life are the same thing. Retirement is not a destination; it is a category error. Happiness is downstream of being aligned with the work; alignment is the cause, happiness the symptom.

?What tools do you need to achieve these goals?

  • The 150-formulation pipeline manufactured reliably and at quality.
  • A single unified data system across formulations, clients, ingredients, and biofield scans.
  • AI integrators (the role Justus fills) so I am not the bottleneck on any operation.
  • A practitioner channel that turns one conversation into hundreds of patients reached.
  • Time-on-calendar protected for writing and formulation — the two highest-leverage uses of an hour.

?What kind of company would give you the things you need for success in life?

This one — Healing Oasis and Remedy Match — once integration is complete. The capability is already here. Every system is identified, paid for, and ready. The constraint is connecting what is already built. The next twelve months are an integration project, not a capability project.

?Your greatest gifts — and what to work on

Gifts. Pattern recognition across decades of clinical data. Synthesis across disciplines — physics, biology, optometry, consciousness research. Calm in the presence of degenerative diagnoses other practitioners flinch from.

To work on. Delegation. Narrowing focus when the pipeline tempts breadth. Allowing systems and teammates to carry weight I have historically carried personally — the same shift from "I" to "We" we ask of every client. It’s the difference between Illness and Wellness.

?How will you know you are on the correct path for your highest good and life?

The body tells you. Symptoms reverse. Clinical indicators move. Vision clears. Sleep deepens. The right people appear at the right time. The work is hard, but it is never depleting in the way wrong work is depleting. Coherence — internal and external — is the signal. Coherence opens the gate to Syntropy, the missing law of physics and the source that overcomes the entropy of aging and degeneration, and governs the self-organizing development of living systems and all self-healing.
