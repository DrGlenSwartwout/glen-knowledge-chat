Frequently asked

Quick answers.

Is there a minimum order?
No. Once your application is approved you can order any quantity. A single bottle is $50, and the whole order re-prices as it fills: about $45 a bottle at one box (20), and $40 at two boxes (40+). Certification lowers that floor by $1.25 a bottle per module, down to $25 at volume once you complete all twelve. Mix-and-match across the full 150+ formulation catalog.
How do I unlock the healing tools at 50% off?
Tool pricing unlocks when you initiate ASH Certification training. Initiation — not completion — is the threshold, because the training itself is what makes the tools clinically useful in your practice.
Can I use these alongside the supplement lines I already carry?
Yes — and that's how most new practitioners start. Think of Functional Formulations™ as your clinical escalation option: the line you reach for when your usual protocols haven't moved a case. Practitioners typically begin by using us for their hardest 10–20% of cases, then expand into broader dispensing as they see the response patterns.
Can you drop-ship to my clients?
Yes — once approved, drop-ship to client addresses is supported. Rae will walk you through the order workflow during your welcome conversation.
What about international orders?
USPS ships to US addresses only. For international practitioners, we recommend a US-based shipping forwarder; the specific service we suggest depends on your country. Rae will recommend a forwarder in her welcome reply, and Wise is our recommended payment rail for international transfers.
How do the referrals actually work?
As we expand commercial advertising, inbound consumer leads who ask "where do I find this in my area?" are routed to active panel practitioners — matched by geography and, where relevant, modality. Practitioners on the panel are also listed in the Practitioner Directory used by our customer service team.
What's the return policy on tools and formulations?
Formulations are non-returnable once shipped (consumable products). Tools come with the manufacturer's warranty and a 30-day functional defect return window — Rae will walk through specifics when an order is placed.
How long does application review take?
One to two business days, typically. Rae reviews each application personally, replies with a welcome note covering ordering, shipping, and (if you're interested) ASH Certification next steps.
